darack Privacy Policy

darack does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All content you add (recommendations, people, notes) is stored exclusively on your device using local storage. No analytics, no crash reporting, no advertising, no third-party SDKs with data collection are used.

To display artwork, the app sends item titles to the iTunes Search API (a public Apple service). No personal information is included in these requests.

When you share a URL to darack from another app, the share extension may contact the URL's host service (such as Spotify or YouTube) to look up the title of the content. This is a direct, real-time request from your device; no data passes through darack's servers and nothing is retained beyond completing the lookup.

To delete your data, uninstall the app.
